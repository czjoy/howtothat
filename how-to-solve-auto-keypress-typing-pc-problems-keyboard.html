How to solve auto key press problem or other keyboard malware problems

 In this article i am going to solve auto key press problem like [[[[[[, ////////, ppppppp, *****

etc different unwanted key press problem.

Why this problem occur:

This problem mainly occur due to viruses, malwares etc. So try not to use malware software and other cracked software that harmful your PC.

How can you solve this:

there are different way that you can solve this. First method is to disable your keyboard for some days and enable your keyboard. 80% probability is that your problem will solve. But if the process does not work then there is a another process which is little bit  data loss but 100% effective is to change your operating system.

If your keyboard is not hardware damaged then this process will work for you.

Now lets talk about first method how you disable your keyboard:

NB: Before doing this you need external keyboard mouse. If you have not any external mouse and keyboard don't do this. you can borrow from your friend.

Step 1:

Go to this PC or search in windows search section 'device manager'. Or you can follow this step in the picture.

 

Step 2:

Then go to device manager see the following picture. 

This PC -->  Computer-->Manage.


Step 3:

Go to device manager--> and right click on standard Ps2 Keyboard --> then click on Update Driver Software.


step 4:

then select Browse my computer for driver software


step 5:

After that click on --> Let me pick from a list of device drivers on my computer

step 6:

Now there is option called Show compatible hardware uncheck this --> then Chose any driver for example i chose the toshiba driver --> then doule click on Toshiba USB 100 Japanese keyboard --then click next.

step 7:

After this you need to restart your computer then your keyboard and mouse pad of your laptop will be disabled. Now you can notice that your keyboard auto typing is solved. Now plug your external mouse so that you can work, Now how can you enable it back.

step 8:

After disable your keyboard you need to wait for at least 2 to 3 days. For enabling this you need extra mouse and keyboard now follow this process. Just go to Device Manager --> goto keyboard--> right click the driver that you install. So i install the Toshiba driver so right click it ---> and uninstall --> then restart your PC that's it. your keyboard will enable again.


After disabling your keyboard you need to to keep disable it for 2 to 3 days. if this not work try to change to another driver in step 6. If try this process and also change the driver but not work then, maybe your laptop PC is full with lots virus and malware. So you can change your Operating system.

Changing operating system, you not going to loss your personal data just you need to shift all your file from c drive to other drive. And after reinstall your OS, you need install all of your software again, you can see YouTube tutorials about this.
